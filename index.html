01 ABOUT

I build AI that runs close to home: agents, retrieval and voice, on your machine, not someone else's cloud.

I'm Mohamed Azarudeen, a software engineer who builds AI systems end to end: the pipeline, the model integration, the API, and the interface it all lives in.

I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, where I also work as a Research & Student Assistant. Before that, I worked as a Software Engineer Intern at LuminaView in New York, and as a Software Engineer I at Mavdero Techservices in Chennai, India.

The through-line in my work is local-first AI: systems powered by lightweight local LLMs, RAG pipelines with cited sources, multi-agent orchestration, and interfaces that make all of it feel effortless.

Blog to Podcast Agent
